The High Cost of Traditional Maintenance

Industrial operations are plagued by inefficiencies that lead to massive losses. The current approach is broken.

Unplanned Downtime

Reactive maintenance leads to unexpected failures, halting production and causing significant financial and operational disruption.

Excessive Costs

Scheduled maintenance is often unnecessary, leading to wasted resources. Physical sensors are expensive to buy, install, and maintain.

Deployment Complexity

Contact-based sensors require physical installation on each machine, a complex and often hazardous process that scales poorly.

The Future is Contactless

VibraSense revolutionizes industrial monitoring by replacing physical sensors with smart, vision-based analysis. We leverage existing or high-speed cameras to see what the human eye can't, identifying micro-vibrations that signal impending failure.

  • Scalable & Non-Invasive: Monitor entire factory floors without touching a single machine.

  • Cost-Effective: Leverage existing camera infrastructure or low-cost high-speed cameras.

  • Highly Accurate: Our AI models detect faults like imbalance, friction, and cracks with remarkable precision.

Our Technical Pipeline

We transform raw video into actionable intelligence through a sophisticated, multi-stage process.

01

Video Capture

Record machinery at 100-1000+ FPS to capture subtle micro-vibrations.

02

Motion Magnification

Amplify imperceptible pixel movements to make hidden vibrations visible.

03

Signal Extraction

Convert pixel intensity changes into time-series vibration data.

04

Fourier Transform (FFT)

Transform signals into the frequency domain to isolate patterns.

05

Feature Extraction

Identify key statistical descriptors like peak frequency, entropy, and energy.

06

AI Inference

Feed features into ML models to classify faults and predict Remaining Useful Life (RUL).
